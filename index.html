Tricia Morey

Accident Reconstructionist

Tricia Morey is an Accident Reconstruction Consultant with over 15 years experience. She has been a licensed Professional Engineer in the state of Texas since 2012. She received a Masters and Bachelors degree in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Florida. Tricia's primary focus is on creating animations, simulations, and analysis for court room presentations of passenger vehicles and heavy trucks accidents, but she also has experience reconstructing motorcycle, bicycle, scooter, and pedestrian involved incidents. Before becoming a full time Accident Reconstruction Consultant, she worked for Solar Turbines, a Caterpillar Company, for over 12 years designing gas turbines and large scale gas refining facilities for oil and gas, and power generation applications.
